Know The League

* Each team comprises of 12 players. The captain/mentor of the team is chosen directly by the team owner. The uniqueness of the league lies in the fact that 7 players per team are amateurs that are selected through on-ground / online qualifiers and other contests.

*For Season 3, there will be 24 free on-ground qualifiers (Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Bengaluru) - which are basically free "live" poker events (Schedule to be announced end November) - where participants have to play multiple poker tournaments to "earn" points (based on their position in the tournaments).

*A nation wide leaderboard will be maintained and the players who earn the maximum points make it to the "draft".

*Post Qualifiers, there will be a selection ceremony, where the team owners & mentors will choose the players from the various drafts, which will comprise of the final team. Selection Ceremony will be in Delhi on March 1st, 2020

*Once the teams are selected and finalized, the players undergo training for the next 3 weeks so that mentors/coaches can identify the weaknesses in players and prepare them for the finale. All selected players get a base salary as well as performance incentive (if the teams finish in the top 4 positions in the finale)

*The Poker Sports League grand finale will be in Kolkata in from March 15-23rd, 2020.

*The total prize-pool will be Rs 4 + crores, with the top 4 teams being paid based on their finishing position.

* All team members also get a percentage of the winning prize (predetermined split).
